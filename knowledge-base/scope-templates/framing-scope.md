# Standard Scope Language — Framing
# Preferred Builders General Services Inc.
# Use as reference for generating scope descriptions

## STANDARD FRAMING SCOPE (New Construction)

### WHAT IS INCLUDED:
- Layout and installation of all exterior wall framing per approved plans
- 2×6 exterior walls at 16" O.C. (or 2×4 as specified)
- Interior partition walls per plan
- All floor framing systems (TJI, dimensional lumber, or LVL as specified)
- Roof framing system (rafters, trusses, or hybrid as specified)
- Structural LVL beams and headers per engineer's specifications
- Window and door rough openings
- Stair framing (rough stairs — finished stairs separate)
- Blocking for future grab bars (bathrooms — MA code)
- Fire blocking and draftstopping per code
- Sheathing — walls (OSB or plywood per plan) and roof deck
- Hurricane ties and structural connectors (per code)
- Temporary bracing during construction
- Cleanup of framing debris

### WHAT IS NOT INCLUDED:
- Engineered drawings or structural engineering (owner to supply stamped plans)
- Exterior sheathing wrap/housewrap (separate line)
- Roof underlayment (roofing contractor)
- Windows and doors (separate line)
- Decking on decks (separate — framing only in this line)
- Finish stairs or railings
- Any work below grade

---

## GARAGE FRAMING SCOPE

### WHAT IS INCLUDED:
- Sill plate installation on slab (anchor bolts by foundation contractor)
- Exterior wall framing (2×6 at 16" O.C. standard)
- Headers for overhead door openings (double LVL per span)
- Roof framing system as specified
- Wall sheathing (OSB)
- Roof deck sheathing (OSB)
- Fire blocking between garage and living space (if attached or below)

### WHAT IS NOT INCLUDED:
- Overhead garage doors or openers (separate line)
- Insulation of garage walls (excluded unless specified)
- Drywall in garage (excluded unless specified — required by code on garage/living wall)

---

## DECK FRAMING SCOPE

### WHAT IS INCLUDED:
- Ledger board attachment to structure (flashed)
- Pressure-treated posts on appropriate footings
- PT girders and joists per span tables
- Blocking and rim boards
- Joist hangers and structural connectors
- No deck boards, railing, or stairs unless specified

## COMMON FRAMING SPECIFICATIONS

| Item | Standard Spec | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| Exterior walls | 2×6 @ 16" O.C. | Allows R-20 insulation |
| Interior partitions | 2×4 @ 16" O.C. | Bath walls 2×6 for plumbing |
| Floor joists | 11 7/8" TJI @ 16" O.C. | Or dimensional per span |
| Ridge beam | Structural LVL | Sized by engineer |
| Rafters | 2×10 or 2×12 @ 16" O.C. | Per roof load/span |
| Roof sheathing | 5/8" OSB | H-clips at unsupported edges |
| Wall sheathing | 7/16" OSB or 1/2" CDX | |
| Sill plate | 2× PT lumber | On concrete, anchor bolted |
| LVL headers | 1-3/4" × depth per span | Doubled at garage openings |

## HOW TO DESCRIBE FRAMING IN A PROPOSAL

Example language for scope section:

**Framing — Structure**
Complete rough framing of [X] sqft [building type] per approved plans. 
Work includes all exterior walls (2×6 @ 16" O.C.), interior partitions, 
[floor system: 11 7/8" TJI floor joists], roof system ([type] with 
structural LVL ridge beam), window and door rough openings, fire blocking, 
and wall + roof deck sheathing. All work per Massachusetts State Building 
Code and approved structural drawings.

**Framing — Deck**
Framing of [X sqft] exterior deck including PT ledger, posts, girders, 
and joists. Deck boards, railing, and stairs by separate allowance/contract.
