# Massachusetts Residential Contract Requirements
# HIC Law — 780 CMR + M.G.L. c. 142A
# Reference for Preferred Builders contract generation

## MANDATORY CONTRACT ELEMENTS (MA Law)

Under M.G.L. c. 142A and 780 CMR, all residential contracts over $1,000 MUST include:

1. **Contractor name, address, registration number**
   - Preferred Builders General Services Inc.
   - 37 Duck Mill Road, Fitchburg, MA 01420
   - LIC# HIC-197400

2. **Customer name and property address**

3. **Detailed description of work** (scope of work by trade)

4. **Total contract price** — must be stated clearly

5. **Payment schedule** — must be tied to milestones, NOT arbitrary dates
   - Deposit: maximum 1/3 of total at signing (we use 33%)
   - Draw schedule: tied to completion milestones
   - Final payment: not less than 5% withheld until completion

6. **Start date and substantial completion date** (or good faith estimate)

7. **Right of Rescission Notice** — customer has 3 business days to cancel
   - Must be on a separate, signed page
   - Must state cancellation rights in plain language

8. **Permit responsibility statement** — who pulls permits

9. **Warranty statement** — 1 year minimum on labor (MA implied warranty)

10. **Arbitration/dispute resolution clause** (optional but recommended)

11. **Change order procedure** — all changes must be in writing

12. **Subcontractor disclosure** — right to list major subs

## PAYMENT SCHEDULE STRUCTURE (Preferred Builders Standard)

| Milestone | Payment |
|-----------|---------|
| Contract signing (deposit) | 33% |
| Foundation complete | 15% |
| Framing complete | 20% |
| Rough mechanicals (electric/plumbing/HVAC) | 15% |
| Insulation & drywall complete | 10% |
| Final completion (punch list done) | 7% |

**Note:** Final payment (7%) held until customer walk-through and punch list complete.

## STANDARD WARRANTY LANGUAGE (MA Compliant)

Preferred Builders warrants all labor and workmanship for one (1) year from the date of 
substantial completion. Manufacturer warranties apply to all materials and equipment as 
supplied. This warranty does not cover damage caused by customer misuse, acts of God, 
normal wear and tear, or modifications made by others.

---

## RIGHT OF RESCISSION (Required Language)

"You may cancel this contract within three (3) business days of signing. To cancel, 
deliver written notice to: Preferred Builders General Services Inc., 37 Duck Mill Road, 
Fitchburg, MA 01420. If you cancel, any property traded in, any payments made, and any 
negotiable instruments executed will be returned. Any security interest arising out of 
the transaction will be cancelled."

---

## LIEN WAIVER LANGUAGE

Upon receipt of each payment, Preferred Builders shall provide a partial lien waiver 
covering work completed through that payment. Upon final payment, a full and final 
lien waiver shall be provided.

---

## FORCE MAJEURE CLAUSE

Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by circumstances beyond their reasonable 
control, including but not limited to: acts of God, severe weather, material shortages, 
supply chain disruptions, labor disputes, government orders, or pandemic conditions. 
Schedule adjustments due to force majeure shall not constitute a breach of contract.

---

## DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any dispute arising from this contract shall first be subject to good-faith negotiation 
between the parties. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to 
mediation through the American Arbitration Association before litigation. The prevailing 
party in any legal action shall be entitled to reasonable attorney's fees.

---

## INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

Preferred Builders shall maintain:
- General Liability: minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence
- Workers Compensation: as required by MA law
- Certificates of insurance available upon request

Customer is responsible for maintaining homeowner's insurance throughout construction.

---

## CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES STANDARD LANGUAGE

Customer shall:
1. Provide clear, safe access to the work site during normal business hours (7am–5pm M–F)
2. Remove personal property from work areas prior to start
3. Make timely decisions on allowance selections (by framing completion)
4. Make payments per the payment schedule
5. Not engage other contractors in the same work area during construction without written consent
6. Provide temporary utilities (water/electric) if primary service is unavailable
7. Obtain HOA approval if applicable prior to work start

---

## PERMIT LANGUAGE

Preferred Builders shall apply for and obtain all required building permits. Permit fees 
are included in the contract price as itemized. Customer shall not request inspections 
or communicate with the building department regarding this project without prior 
coordination with Preferred Builders. All work shall be performed in accordance with 
the Massachusetts State Building Code (780 CMR) and all applicable local amendments.
