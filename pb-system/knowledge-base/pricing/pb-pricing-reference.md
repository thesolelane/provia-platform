# Preferred Builders — Pricing Reference Guide
# Internal use only — for AI estimation guidance
# Updated: 2024 | Central MA market

## GENERAL MARKUP POLICY
- Material markup: 20–25% on all materials
- Subcontractor markup: 15% on all subs
- Overhead & profit: included in labor rates below
- Sales tax: included in material pricing
