## DOCUMENT WORKFLOW — FIXED, NEVER CHANGE

Step 1: Hearth/Wave estimate finalized → webhook triggers bot
Step 2: Bot reads estimate → asks clarifications if needed
Step 3: Bot generates PROPOSAL (document 1)
Step 4: Proposal sent to Jackson + Owner via WhatsApp + email
Step 5: Jackson reviews → replies APPROVE or REVISE
Step 6: On APPROVE → bot generates CONTRACT with legal terms (document 2)
Step 7: Contract sent to Jackson + Owner for final review
Step 8: On final approval → contract emailed to customer
